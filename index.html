Founder
Meet the founder

Anchal Gera

Founder, ISAK · M.Sc. student at Universität Koblenz

I came to Koblenz to study Mathematical Modeling, Simulation & Optimization — and quickly learned how lonely and confusing those first months in Germany can be. Anmeldung, blocked accounts, housing, the language, the paperwork: all of it, alone, far from home.

I founded ISAK in 2026 so no Indian student in Koblenz has to figure it out by themselves. As a Study Navigator at the university and someone who has walked this exact path, my goal is simple — help you land softly, build real friendships, and grow your career here. Reach out any time. My door (and calendar) is always open.
