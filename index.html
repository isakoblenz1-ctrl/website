Why ISAK

A community, not
a paperwork desk.

Moving across continents is hard. ISAK exists to make Koblenz feel less foreign — from your first Anmeldung to your last Bachelorarbeit, and every Diwali in between.

Soft landing

Airport pickup coordination, first-week SIM card help, and residence permit walkthroughs from seniors who just did it.

Festivals & culture

Diwali, Holi, Independence Day, Onam, Navratri — celebrated loudly, together. Our Diwali Night sells out the Audimax every year.

03

Career & networks

Alumni connections, partner company access, CV labs, interview prep, and German-language conversation meetups.

04

Mentorship

Seniors who've navigated German bureaucracy, housing, and exams. Real advice, no sugar-coating, whenever you need it.
