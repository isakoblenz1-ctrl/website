Helpful
resources.

We're a new association still preparing our own official documents — so for now, here are the most useful free, official resources for studying and living in Koblenz. We'll add ISAK's own guides as we create them.

Visa & pre-arrival (from India)

Official sources for the student visa, APS certificate and blocked account.
WEB

German Missions in India — Student visa

Official visa information, appointment booking and document checklists from the German embassy/consulates in India.

india.diplo.de
WEB

APS India certificate

The Academic Evaluation Centre certificate now required for Indian students applying to German universities.

aps-india.de
WEB

Blocked account (Sperrkonto) — explained

What a blocked account is, how much you need, and approved providers. From DAAD's official Study in Germany portal.

study-in-germany.de
WEB

Make it in Germany

The German government's official portal for studying, working and living in Germany.

make-it-in-germany.com

Universities & living in Koblenz

Your international offices, the city, housing and getting around.
WEB

Universität Koblenz — International Office

Support for international students: enrolment, orientation and advice.

uni-koblenz.de
WEB

Hochschule Koblenz — International

International office and services for students at the university of applied sciences.

hs-koblenz.de
WEB

Stadt Koblenz — Bürgeramt (Anmeldung)

Register your address and find city services. Anmeldung is one of your first must-dos.

koblenz.de
WEB

Studierendenwerk Koblenz — Housing

Student dormitories, dining and support services in Koblenz.

studierendenwerk-koblenz.de
WEB

WG-Gesucht — Shared flats & rooms

Germany's most-used site for finding a WG or apartment. Watch for scams — never pay before viewing.

wg-gesucht.de
WEB

VRM — Public transport & semester ticket

Bus and train info for the Rhein-Mosel region, including your semester ticket.

vrminfo.de

Health insurance & money

Public health insurance for students and where to learn more.
WEB

Techniker Krankenkasse (TK)

Popular public health insurer with English service — a common choice for international students.

tk.de
WEB

AOK Rheinland-Pfalz/Saarland

Regional public health insurer covering Koblenz.

aok.de
WEB

Study in Germany (DAAD)

The official one-stop portal for planning your studies, costs, scholarships and life in Germany.

study-in-germany.de
