questions.

Can't find the answer? Email us at hello@isak-koblenz.de and someone on the board will reply within 48 hours.

Is ISAK membership really free?
Yes — completely free for all enrolled students at Hochschule Koblenz (both campuses) as well as students at nearby universities. ISAK is a non-profit Verein that runs on voluntary contributions and sponsorship income. We will never charge members for basic membership.
I haven't arrived in Koblenz yet. Can I join now?
Absolutely. Many students join ISAK weeks or even months before they arrive in Germany. You'll get added to the pre-arrival WhatsApp group where seniors answer questions about housing, paperwork, packing lists, and everything else. When you land, ISAK already feels like home.
I'm not Indian — can I still join or attend events?
Yes to both. ISAK events are open to everyone — most events don't require membership at all. We love welcoming German students, international students from other countries, and anyone curious about Indian culture. Membership is open to anyone who wants to be part of the community, regardless of nationality.
What do I get as a member that non-members don't?
Access to our members-only WhatsApp groups, early access and priority registration for workshops and ticketed events, members-only resources (CV templates, housing guides as we build them), and a real say in how this new community grows. Most of all: people who have your back in Koblenz.
How long does it take to activate my membership?
Instantly! Your welcome email — with the WhatsApp group invite and your member ID — is sent the moment you submit the form. A real person from ISAK then follows up personally within 48 hours on weekdays. We're volunteers with exam schedules too, so occasionally it's a little longer — but you're in the community from the second you join.
Can I volunteer or help organise events?
We actively encourage it. After joining as a member, mention in your first email or at the next open office hours that you'd like to help. You don't need to run for the board — we have a volunteer pool for event setup, photography, workshops, and social media. It's a great way to build a German-compatible CV entry too.
