Want to lead?

Elections every
January.

Any member who has been with ISAK for at least one full semester can stand for any board position. No experience needed — just commitment and enthusiasm.

1
November
Nomination period opens. Express your interest to the current board.
2
December
Candidate introductions and Q&A at the General Assembly.
3
January
Electronic vote. New board takes over from February 1st.
