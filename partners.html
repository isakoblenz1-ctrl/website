Why partner with ISAK

Reach students who
will stay.

ISAK members are ambitious, technically skilled, and actively seeking long-term careers in Germany. They are precisely the talent Germany's Mittelstand needs.

Direct student reach

A growing community of Indian students across M.Sc., B.Eng. and MBA programmes at both Koblenz universities.

Motivated talent

Ambitious students in engineering, IT, logistics and management — actively looking to build careers in Germany.

On-campus presence

Booth space at Welcome Days, career fairs, and dedicated event slots to meet students directly.

Newsletter & social

Featured placement in our newsletter and posts to our growing Instagram community.

Partnership process

How it
works.

01

Get in touch

Email us at hello@isak-koblenz.de or fill out the contact form. We'll respond within 2 working days.

02

Receive the deck

We'll send you our Partnership Brochure 2026 with demographics, event calendar, and detailed package breakdowns.

03

Sign the MoU

Our treasury and your team agree on scope and sign a simple Memorandum of Understanding. No lengthy legal processes.

04

Go live

Your logo and presence go live at the next event. We send a welcome announcement to our full membership.
